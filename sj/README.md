# Stylized Johansen Model
The model is based on Chapter 3: The Johansen Approach in Notes and Problems in Applied General Equilibrium Economics by Dixon et al. 

## The theoretical structure for the Stylized Johansen model
The model has two commodities, two primary factors and one final user (the household sector). We denote 0 for household, 1 and 2 for each commodity, and 3 and 4 for primary factors, labor and capital.  Each industry produce only its commodity (Hence, there are two industries).

## Household
The household sector chooses its consumption level of goods 1 and 2 ($X_{10}$ and $X_{20}$)to maximize the Cobb-Douglas utility function
$$
    U = X_{10}^{\alpha_{10}}X_{20}^{\alpha_{20}} \text{s.t.} P_1X_{10} + P_2X_{20} = Y
$$
Hence, the household demand functions are
$$
    X_{c0} = \frac{\alpha_{c0}Y}{P_c}, \qquad c=1,2
$$
